KAIST Tag Fortune

Tap your card. Receive your fortune.

✦

About the Project

KAIST Tag Fortune is a lightweight NFC interaction installed near the building entrance. When students tap their access card to enter, a small prompt invites them to also tap the NFC reader beside the door — and in return, they receive a short fortune for the day. The intervention takes under three seconds, adds no friction to the existing flow, and turns a routine gesture into a moment of pause and small delight.

The web experience mirrors that physical slip of paper: a single fortune appears on a warm, aged-paper card against a dark background. Each tag generates a unique fortune and a lucky color, both derived from the tag token so the result is consistent but unpredictable across different cards.

Context
